    Platform pillars

    Everything a land buyer needs before making the leap.

    The concept combines listing discovery, parcel intelligence, local service referrals, and a practical readiness score for people leaving city life behind.

    Land intelligence

    Water, septic viability, power access, buildable percentage, grading needs, average slope, parcel number, zoning, and tax history presented in plain English.

    Homesteading viability

    Monthly weather summaries, rainfall, wind severity, and climate suitability for gardening, livestock, and seasonal access.

    Rural convenience

    Distances to the nearest city, hardware store, Costco, hospital, and emergency response corridor so buyers can balance privacy with practicality.

    Local resource marketplace

    Connect prospects with trusted well drillers, solar installers, builders, septic experts, fencing crews, and site prep contractors near each parcel.

    Buyer decision support

    Homestead readiness score

    A transparent weighted model helps buyers compare rural properties beyond generic real estate filters.

    Core score categories

    Recommended parcel fields

    • Parcel number and county records link
    • Water source status and well depth nearby
    • Septic suitability / perc test status
    • Utility access and estimated hookup distance
    • Percent buildable under slope thresholds
    • Average slope and cut/fill leveling estimate
    • Zoning, overlays, setbacks, and permitted uses
    • Tax history, annual carrying costs, and exemptions
    • Distance to city, hospital, hardware, groceries, Costco
    • Rainfall, temperature bands, wind, growing season
    Trusted local network

    Pair every property with vetted rural service providers

    Turn a listing portal into an execution platform by helping buyers estimate and source the work required to develop raw land.

    Site setup

    Excavation, grading, driveway access, retaining work, fencing, and pads.

    Utilities & water

    Well drilling, water storage, solar, generators, batteries, septic, propane.

    Structures

    Cabins, barndominiums, frame builders, greenhouses, barns, sheds, and workshops.

    Land use support

    Surveyors, soil testing, permaculture designers, animal enclosures, and feed suppliers.
